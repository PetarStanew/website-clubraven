Heritage in Every Glass

Our Story

Since 1946

Enduring since 1946, CLUB RAVEN has outlasted trends because it was built on people: neighborhood regulars, first dates, and celebrations that became tradition.

The club opened its doors in 1946 as a quiet refuge for returning neighbors. Over decades, we have welcomed musicians, artists, and the city itself. Every seat is a front-row view to Sacramento's midnight heartbeat.
